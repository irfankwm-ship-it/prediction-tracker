Our Mission

Democratizing information for an informed citizenry.

For too long, understanding our world has been a privilege reserved for governments, corporations, and the wealthy. While ordinary citizens are expected to vote, invest, and make life decisions, the intelligence that shapes global events remains locked behind paywalls, classified briefings, and insider networks.

We believe this is fundamentally wrong.

Know What Matters exists to democratize information. We harness the same open sources that professional analysts use—satellite imagery, trade data, conflict monitors, and diplomatic channels—and synthesize them into actionable briefings that anyone can understand.

KWM Intel is a project of Know What Matters focused on providing professional-level global security intelligence for free to global citizens. KWM Intel is not a business model. It's a mission.

Because an informed citizenry isn't a luxury—it's the foundation of democracy itself.
